About the project

The "Lofi Mood" project is a Telegram bot developed with the aim of elevating you to the soothing and cozy world of lofi music. Lofi, short for "low fidelity," is a distinct music genre characterized by gentle melodies, soft rhythms, and an abundance of atmospheric sounds. This genre is considered an ideal background for creativity, work, reading, or simply unwinding. The goal of "Lofi Mood" is to make the music selection process even more convenient and intuitive. The bot analyzes your current mood based on your responses to a brief questionnaire and suggests the most suitable playlist that perfectly captures your emotional state. Additionally, "Lofi Mood" offers the ability to save the created playlists in your Spotify account, ensuring instant access to the music at your convenience.
